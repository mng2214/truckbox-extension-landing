Privacy Policy

The Truck Box extension helps you send broker emails from one.dat.com using your own Gmail account via OAuth. We designed it to work locally in your browser and keep data collection to a minimum.

Information we collect

  • No personal data is sent to our servers. We do not run any backend for this extension.
  • Local extension settings. Your name/MC/phone and email templates are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device.
  • On-page content you see. The extension reads visible fields on the DAT page (e.g., company email, origin, destination, pickup date) to populate your email template. This parsing happens in your browser only.
  • Google OAuth tokens. Tokens are issued and cached by Chrome via chrome.identity. We do not receive or store your tokens.

How we use information

  • Sending email via Gmail API. With your consent, the extension uses the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send to send the composed email from your Gmail. No reading of inbox content occurs.
  • Quality & troubleshooting. We do not collect analytics or crash reports. If you contact support, we'll only use the info you provide to help you.
OAuth scopes used: gmail.send, openid, email, profile. We request the minimum necessary to authenticate and send email.

Storage & security

  • Local-only settings. Templates and profile fields are kept in your browser storage. Remove them anytime (see "Data deletion").
  • Token handling. OAuth tokens are handled by Chrome and transmitted only to Google's Gmail API to send your message.
  • No sale or sharing. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data deletion & revoking access

  • Revoke Google access: visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove access for "Truck Box".
  • Clear local data: open chrome://extensions → remove the extension, or from the extension popup use any provided "Reset to defaults" action (if available).
  • Uninstall: removing the extension deletes its local storage.
  • Need help? Contact us .

Your rights (CPRA/GDPR)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Since data is not sent to our servers, requests typically involve revoking Google access and/or removing the extension. Contact us if you need assistance.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page as the extension evolves. We'll update the "Last updated" date above and, when appropriate, notify users within the extension.

Compliance: Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use)

When you connect Google, the extension's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • Purpose-limited: Gmail data is used only to send the email you asked the extension to send.
  • No human access: No humans read your Gmail data.
  • No sharing: Gmail data is not transferred except to provide the feature you requested (sending via Gmail API).
  • No advertising: Gmail data is not used for ads or profiling.
  • Data minimization: We request gmail.send only; we do not read your messages or mailbox.

If you revoke access, the extension will not be able to send emails via Gmail until you reconnect.
